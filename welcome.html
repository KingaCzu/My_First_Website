About us

Providing high quality embroidery and screen- printing services in the heart of Manchester.

Based in the industrial part of Manchester 2BM offers its clients high quality embroidering and screen-printing services. We have over 10 years of experience in the industry and our clients can benefit from our unique embroidery expertise. We understand that every business and enquiry differ and therefore we offer personalised and flexible approach to our clients needs.

Products we use:
  1. 2BM uses Happy Multi-head Embroidery Machines known for its reliability and speed to ensure that our customers orders are produced to an excellent standard and delivered in a timely manner.
  2. Our threads are supplied by Madeira, the market leader in the machine embroidery threads, providing the highest quality of embroidery.
  3. 2BM can also provide great selection of garment for your embroidery or screen-printing project/event and we are proud of working with well-established producers and garment suppliers.
At 2BM we care about our customers, small and big making sure that each order is completed to an exceptional standard. Our company values reflect what we believe in: sustainability, reliability and efficiency.
